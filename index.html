Budding is an app for measuring and tracking the progress of plants using a photograph and the pot height measurement, made in two weeks as the final group project for the Northcoders coding bootcamp course.

The user places markers on the photo at the bottom of the pot, the top of the pot, and top of the plant. The app uses the position of these markers to calculate the distance between the bottom and top of the pot in pixels, and create a pixel-to-cm scale using the real-life pot height. It then uses this scale to calculate the real-life height of the plant. Each time a user measures their plant, it is saved as a snapshot and can be viewed on the plant page. Measurements are displayed on a chart, enabling the tracking of plant growth over time.

A Reddit-style discussion website with articles and comments which users can choose a login, up vote and down vote, browse by topic, and sort by date or most popular.

Made as coursework for the Northcoders bootcamp, the site is responsive and made with accessibility in mind, including semantic HTML, suitable colour contrasts, focus indication, ARIA labels, and tested using the Chrome Vox screen reader.

The site is served by a purpose-built, fully tested RESTful API, also made as part of the Northcoders bootcamp, and hosted with Heroku.
